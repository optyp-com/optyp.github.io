Refund Policy

At OptyP, we strive to deliver exceptional resume and profile optimization that meets your expectations. If you are unsatisfied, please review the refund policy terms below.

Last updated: September 30, 2025

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered in the following cases:

  • Timeframe: Requests made within 7 days of service delivery.
  • Service Issues: Undelivered services or unresolved quality concerns.
  • Non-Delivery: Failure to deliver on agreed timelines without notice.
  • Technical Errors: Critical errors impacting service functionality.

Services Not Eligible for Refund

  • Services completed and customer approved.
  • Change of personal preference post delivery.
  • Dissatisfaction with job search results.
  • Services used or accessed beyond 7-day period.
  • Custom requests outside original package scope.

Refund Process

Step-by-Step Refund Procedure

  1. Submit Request: Contact us at info@optyp.com.
  2. Provide Details: Include order number, package details, and refund reason.
  3. Review: Our team reviews your request within 5 business days.
  4. Resolution: We communicate our decision and process refund within 7-10 business days.

Information Needed for Refund Requests

  • Full name and email used for purchase
  • Order/Transaction ID
  • Purchased package/service type
  • Detailed refund reason
  • Supporting documents (if applicable)

Alternative Solutions

Before requesting a refund, consider these options:

  • Free Revisions: Two rounds included in all packages to address concerns.
  • Direct Support: Reach out to discuss and resolve your issues.
  • Consultation Call: Schedule a call to review deliverables.
  • Service Adjustments: Modify scope to better suit needs.

Partial Refund Policy

Partial refunds may be issued upon review in cases such as:

  • Partial delivery due to client unavailability
  • Mid-project scope changes
  • Issues limited to specific bundled services

Partial refund amounts are determined on a case-by-case basis and communicated during the review process.
