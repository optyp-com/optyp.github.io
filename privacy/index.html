Privacy Policy

At OptyP, your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: September 30, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect the following personal and usage data to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Identification: names, contact details including email and phone number.
  • Profile Data: resume content, LinkedIn and other profile details needed for optimization.
  • Usage Information: website interactions and analytics collected via cookies and similar technologies.
  • Communications: correspondence and feedback you provide through email, chat, or forms.

How We Use Your Information

Your data enables us to:

  • Deliver and tailor resume and profile optimization services.
  • Communicate project details and provide customer support.
  • Process payments securely and issue invoices.
  • Analyze service usage to enhance our offerings.
  • Comply with legal requirements and prevent fraudulent activities.
We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing without your explicit consent.

Data Security

We implement advanced safeguards including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmissions.
  • Secure storage with controlled access.
  • Routine audits and system updates.
  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill service obligations, maintain legal compliance, and resolve disputes.

Your Rights

You have rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request copies of your data.
  • Correction: Require amendments to inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request removal of your personal data.
  • Portability: Obtain data for use with other services.
  • Consent Withdrawal: Revoke permission for personal data processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@optyp.com.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in laws or our operations. Significant updates will be posted on our website.

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

Reach out anytime with your concerns or inquiries regarding our data practices.
